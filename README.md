# Yeboah Pendulum Rig Simulator

## Overview
This repository contains the **Yeboah Pendulum Rig Simulator**, a digital repository and simulation environment for exploring the mechanics of mechanical separation. It provides a geometric framework for understanding how pendulum-based impact systems interact with various materials.

## Research Background
The Pendulum Rig is the primary experimental model used to validate **Yeboah’s Law of Cutting**. By modeling the arc and impact force of a pendulum, this simulator helps predict:
- Material separation efficiency.
- Tool wear and resistance.
- Energy conservation in mechanical cutting.

## Mathematical Framework
The simulator utilizes geometric modeling to solve for the energy dissipated during the separation phase, represented by the formula:
\[ E_d = f(m, g, h, \theta) \]
*(Where \(E_d\) is energy dissipation and \(\theta\) is the impact angle).*
